ABOUT ME: |



Hi! My name is Myrriame Dosso. I'm a full-stack software developer located in Atlanta, GA. I went to Georgia Southern and recieved my B.S. in Chemistry. I've been an Environmental Chemist for 3 years before I decided to venture into the tech world. I always enjoyed creating/designing websites since I was in grade school. I was only familiar with HTML though so I decided to learn CSS and JavaScript during the pandemic since I was working from home and I ended up really loving it! I ended up applying to a full stack 6 month bootcamp and ended up really enjoying the ins and outs of coding. My coding journey has been like an oceans wave. Smooth, fun and therapeutic sometimes and rough , overwhelming, and harsh other times but refreshing none the less! In my spare time I enjoy traveling, going on walks and trying out new food!
